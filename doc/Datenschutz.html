BonjourBrowser Datenschutzerklärung

BonjourBrowser kontaktiert während des Betriebes keine eigenen Server im Internet – nur wenn eine Applikation zum Browsen ausgewählt wird. .

BonjourBrowser Privacy Statement

BonjourBrowser does not contact own servers on the net. When you select a service in the list, it will be connected (local LAN) – where this service further directs you is out of control for Bonjour Browser.

BonjourBrowser does not collect personal data.

The content of stored data by the called services is out of control for BonjourBrowser (like in a web Browser).

You can delete such data in the settings menu.

BonjourBrowser needs the following rights:

Network

For scanning the services.
